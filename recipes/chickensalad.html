Chicken Salad

Description

This is Easy Avocado Chicken Salad recipe. It is very heatlhy and easy to make. Perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 4 scallions, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • ¼ sweet onion, chopped
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste (Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
